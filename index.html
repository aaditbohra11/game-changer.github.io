Educational Technology and Mobile Learning

This blog focuses on apps and mobile devices that educators can use in the classroom, as well as strategies to integrate them successfully. It provides educational presentations on topics like "Free Educational Android Apps for Teachers" and "Free Math Resources, Lesson Plans and Games." Mobile devices are changing how students learn outside the classroom, and educators and schools need to be aware of how to integrate them properly with lesson plans and curriculum.

College Cures

College Cures is a blog dedicated to the student experience. The content is divided in several categories, including: relationships, work, health, finance, school, partying, and life in college. The blog helps to guide students both during and after their time in college. Founded in 2010, the blog features contributing writers who basically serve as college life advisors.
